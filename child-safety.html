Child Safety Standards - Discover Balochistan

Discover Balochistan is committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users. We have zero tolerance for any form of Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE), child abuse, child endangerment, grooming, trafficking, or any content that harms children.

Our Standards

Users of Discover Balochistan must not:

  • Create, upload, share, distribute, or promote Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM).
  • Engage in child exploitation, abuse, trafficking, or grooming.
  • Encourage, facilitate, or promote sexual activity involving minors.
  • Use the platform for any activity that endangers children.
  • Share content depicting abuse, exploitation, or inappropriate interactions involving minors.

Reporting Violations

Any content or behavior that violates these standards may be removed immediately. Accounts involved in child exploitation, abuse, or CSAE activities may be permanently suspended and reported to appropriate law enforcement authorities where required by law.

Safety Measures

  • We review reports related to child safety.
  • We investigate suspected violations of our policies.
  • We cooperate with legal authorities when required.
  • We take action against accounts that violate these standards.
